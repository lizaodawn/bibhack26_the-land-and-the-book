Project Overview

To evoke the multi-sensory experience of travel, this project presents travelogues textually, visually, and spatially. We explore The Land and the Book by William McClure Thomson (1806-1884), recounting his three-part journey through the Holy Land (Israel, Palestine, Lebanon, Syria and Jordan). The result is an interactive map of the region, offering text-image pairs and route visualisation. Present-day images were added to peek into the vast changes the area went through in the past 150 years.
